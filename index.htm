Predict success of Texas Chainsaw 3D

The two visualizations shown below can help you predict the success of a movie.

The first one shows the number of positive and negative tweets about the movie.

Using the searchbar above you can see the context of how words or strings of words were used in the tweets.

The second visualization shows the average IMDb rating for the movies every cast member appeared in and the opening weekend revenue for each of those movies.

This chart shows the number of positive and negative tweets that were tweeted each up to the release of the movie with intervals of one hour.

This is done by identifying certain words in said tweets. These words are shown when you mouse over each data point.

By clicking on one of those words you get to see how these words are used in the tweets.

The bar on the far right shows the distribution of positive and negative tweets.

This tree map visualization shows the rating for every castmember by taking the average of the movies they previously appeared in (WHERE???).

The size of the cast member's box shows the sum of the opening weekend revenue of their previous movies.

The color of the boxes indicates the rating of this movie where blue means it received a higher rating on IMDb compared to a red one.

The size of a movie's box depicts how its opening weekend revenue compares to other moves the cast member appeared in.

Click to zoom in and out to see additional information about each movie or cast member.
